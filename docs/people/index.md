# People

This section is for publicly identified individuals and public project personas with direct operational relevance.

## Use a people page when
- A public source clearly names a specific person
- A GitHub username, maintainer persona, or other public handle is the clearest supported identifier
- The identity matters to understanding the operation, tooling, or coordination
- The page can separate confirmed facts from inference

## Keep it under groups or ops when
- Reporting only names a crew or shared persona with no clear individual account-level operational relevance
- The identity claim depends on speculation, handle reuse, or weak social-media reporting
- The operation matters more than the individual identity
